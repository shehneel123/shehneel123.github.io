Mental Health

Saha Al-Aqliya

Although physical fitness and health is important, so it mental health.
It is often disregarded, but keeping your mind happy is equally important.

Ola regham minn an al-layaqa badaniya walsha amar mehme ledlec femmen saha al-aqliya.
Yetem tegahlha fe katir minn al-ahiane wilkin al-hafaz ola aqlak saida ola nafs al-quddir minn al-uhmia.

  1. Sometimes, you or someone you may know struggles with depression, anxiety, and stress. During these difficult times, the first step is to calm down. Irrationally acting is dangerous. Figure out the source of your problem, and once identifying it, you can try to improve it. If you are feeling sad, surround yourself with happy people and think happy thoughts. If you enjoy doing something, go do it! Everyone deserves a break.

    2. Fe baad al-ahiane ant oa shakhs qud tarafa iaani minn al-aktab walkelk walleghad. Khallal huthe al-awqat saaba walkhtowa al-awla he tahdeia.   2. Optimism is key. Happy, happy, happy! In any given situation, look at the positive aspects of it. Studies have confirmed that a positive mind generates energy, increases your confidence, motivates you, and overcomes your fears.

    3. Al-tfaul ho al-moftah. Seide seide said! fe ei hala muinah walenzer fe al-jowaneb al-ijabia ledlec. Waqder akdat al-durassat an al-akel al-igabi jold al-taqah wayazid minn thaktake wihfskew wittgelb ola mekhaoufk.

  3. During times of intense stress, go to a quiet place and sit or lay down. If your surroundings are chaotic, this quiet place will be a good place to calm your nerves. Close your eyes, take deep breaths, and focus on blocking out everything around you. Once you feel more relaxed, open your eyes. Identify the cause of your stress, and think of ways to overcome it. It does not have to be anything big; it can just be small ways to lessen the worry.

Sleep

Noum

Sleeping is a critical part of keeping your health. While you sleep, your body is refueling and re-energizing itself. It is also when your body releases growth hormones, so the more you sleep, the chances of you growing are bigger.

Noum ho jaza hasim minn al-hafaz ola sahtak. Aznaa alnome jasmak ho al-tzod balokod weeada tanchit nevsha. Ho asada andama yatlok jasmak hermonat al-namu, ledlec kelma kent tanam, faras nemo lek akbar.

Sometimes when you sleep, you may have a bad dream. These are called nightmares. Often times, you may wake during the night from your nightmare. If you feel scared, take deep breaths to calm yourself, and remind yourself that it is just a dream. If it helps, pray. If it still does not, then go ahead and wake a loved one so that they can be there for you as well. Remember, though, it is just a dream.
