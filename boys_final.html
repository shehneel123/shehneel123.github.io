Boy's Puberty

Sen Al-Bloug Lilbin

Puberty for boys occurs between the ages of 9 and 14.
This is when your body is transitioning into an adult body. Here are the different ways your body is changing:

Wihas al-bloug lolevtian al-thain tatraweh amarham pen 9 senouat wa 14 sanna.
Hudha ho andama jasmak ho alantqal elly jassam al-kabar. Fima lelle al-turq al-mukhtalafa alti yetger baha jasmak:

  • Growth spurts: all your life, girls were generally taller than you. All of a sudden, you are growing taller than them. This is completely normal! Sometimes, you do not grow taller than them. This is completely normal as well! Your height is mostly based on genetics, so however tall your mom or dad is, is probably how tall you will be.

    • Tafarat al-namu: Tawal hayatke kant fittiat amoma attol menk. Fajaas ant tenmo attol munham. Hudha amar tabiai tamama! Fe baad al-ahiane kent la tenmo attol munham. Hudha amar tabiai tamama keddelk! Westned tolek fe al-ghalib ola alam al-warathah ledlec mhama can taweel al-qama amek oa waldke wurbuma ho keef taweel al-qama sov tecon.

  • Hair: you will begin to notice how hair is growing on your cheeks, chin, around your lips, on your chest, your legs, your armpits, and even down there. There is nothing to worry about, this is just your body transitioning to manhood.

    • Al-Shaar: Sov tabadaa fe malaheza keef yenmo al-shaar ola al-khodain ualzakane wahoul schfetike ola sadrke sakike al-ibtine wahti asfel hanak. Les hanak ma yadao laglaqalqa ohada ho majarred jasmak alantqal elly rajoula.

  • Smell: during puberty, as your body is going through changes, you may have noticed how much you are sweating. Sometimes, this causes a bad smell. Make sure to shower whenever possible and keep yourself clean all the time.

    • Raiha: Khallal fetra al-blugh kama jasmak wimmer petgerate qud lahdt kim kent al-taraq. Fe baad al-ahiane ohada jaspeb raiha kariha. Taked minn alasthammam kelma can delk memcna olahfaz ola nevsk nazifa fe kel waqt.

  • Voice: one night, you sound like a baby, and the next morning, you have a totally different voice! This is because your body changing has also affected your vocal chords, causing your voice to become much deeper. Boys get these at different times, so it is fine if you did not experience this. Everyone's voice is different, so do not feel bad if your voice is higher or deeper than your peers.

    • Sot: Leila wahdeh kent tebadou okanha tafel rezee fafi sabah elioum al-talie ledic sot makhtilf tamama! Wethelk laan teggier jasmak qud athert asada al-habbal soutia al-khassa puke memma tespeb fe sotak letzebah amak bakthir. Al-awlad al-hassol ola huthe fe ocate makhtelfah ledlec la bas idha kent lem toage hudha. Sot al-jamia mekhtelfe ledlec la car balso idha can sotak ala oa amak minn akranek.
