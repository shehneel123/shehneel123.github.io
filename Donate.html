Al-Tabra

We have, ultimately, attempted to help these Syrian refugees to the best of our abilities. We hope that this website is of beneficial use to them, and our aim is to help educate them on the basics of every subject.

Luqud haoulna fe nahaya mattaf masadeh hulaa allajin alsourien baafdal ma ledina minn kadrat. Namel an yakon hudha al-muqa mvida lahmou wahdefna ho al-masadeh fe tethequefhem hawl asasyat kel modoa.

There is, however, only so much we can do. Every donation counts, no matter how small. A couple of dollars can buy toothbrushes and save the refugees from lifelong struggles of cavities. Other amounts of money can buy food, blankets, water bottles, and other essentials needed for daily life.

Wama zelke la yemkenna an nefal swe al-kathir. Kel tabara meham mhama can sghira yamken lzogin minn al-dolarat sharaa farshat al-asnan winkadh allajin minn saraat meda al-hayya minn tjawev. Kamiat akhra minn al-mal yamken sharaa almoad ghadhaidh olbataniat ozgajat meiahe veogeerha minn drururiat lazma lahiah alioumia.

Not a single cent will go wasted.

Lin adhia cent wahid.
