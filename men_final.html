Smoking

Madkhanin

Smoking is an incredibly bad habit for your body.
It has so many harmful effects, which are listed below.

Al-tadkhin ho adeh siya beshkel la yisdak lejsmek.
Ladiha al-kathir minn al-athar dharad welty yetem sardha adnah.

  • Increases your chances of cancer, and not just lung cancer but many others.

    • yazid minn faras alisabe balsertane les fakt sertan al-raiya wilkin al-adid minn alakhrine.

  • Leads to premature aging. No one wants early wrinkles or sagging faces as a young adult.

    • Yode elly cheikhoukha al-mubakrah. La ahad yered al-tajaaeed fe waqt mubaker oa an yakon waguh trhall kashab.

  • Weakens your heart, which increases your chances of a heart attack and stroke.

    • Idaf kulpaki memma yazid minn faras alisabe banouba kalabia wescata domagia.

  • Stains your teeth, specifically dark colors and yellow.

    • Wasma ar asnanke wali wageh al-tahdid al-alwan al-dakna walsifra.

  • Many people do not have access to toothbrushes and toothpaste, but do have access to cigarettes. But smoking causes bad breath, and there is no remedy for this.

  • Katir minn al-nas la jesttion al-wassoul elly farshat al-asnan oumajoun al-asnane wilkin lediham emcaneya al-wassoul elly al-sajair. Wilkin al-tadkhin jaspeb raiha al-fum al-karihas walis hanak allaj lahadha.

  • It is terrible for the environment.

    • Ine amar fazia labiia

  • You smoking causes other people around you to secondhand smoke.

    • Al-tadkhin jaspeb alakhrine minn holk latdikhin an ger kasd.

Smoking is addictive. Of course, it is not easy to overcome this habit, but it is possible. Simply following these steps can help you stop the addiction.

Al-tadkhin ho aladman. Wobtebiaa al-hali vinh les minn al-sahl al-tughlab ola huthe al-ada wilkin minn almamkin. Babsata atbaa huthe al-khatawat yamken an tsaadek ola waqef alidman.

  1. Firstly, you have to be serious about this commitment. It is a challenging feat, but you have the determined mindset, everything will be easier.

    2. Ulah yegeb an tecon jada fe hudha al-altzam. Ine omal shaqus wilkin yegeb an yakon ledic aglia thabata.

  2. Keep yourself busy. Take long walks, do extra chores, do anything that will keep your mind off smoking.

    Ibaka nevsk machgola minn khallal al-dhahab lenzha walkiam baamal menzelia idafia walkiam bui shaya minn shaneh an yebki aqlak qabala al-tadkhin.

  3. Avoid hanging around people who smoke. This will only encourage you to pick up the habit once again.

    4. Tejenp al-taska hawl al-nas al-thain yadkhnon. Ohada sov yeshjak fakt ola al-taqat huthe al-adda morra akhra.

  4. Quit smoking one day at a time. Thinking about how you are never going to smoke again will be overwhelming. Instead, take it one step at a time.

    5. Al-iqlaa an al-tadkhin yomah baad yom atkhadh badaa khatwat ladam tataga ola nevsk.

  5. Celebrate the small victories. The small steps will be essential to getting you to your goal.

    6. Ahtval alantasarat sghira. khatwat saghira sticon dururia lathasl ola hadfak.

  6. Every smoke loses a couple years of your lives.

    7. Kel dakhan yifqud badaa senouat minn hayatek.

  7. If you ever feel like smoking again, take a moment to consider the consequences. The results of smoking are only bad.

    8. Idha kent tergeb fe al-tadkhin morra akhriel takhadh lahda landhar fe al-awaqib. Ntayege al-tadkhin siya fakt.

Acne

Habb Chabab

Acne is the result of hormonal changes in the body that causes small red and white bumps to occur on the face. Acne can also be caused by improper care of the face, such as a buildup of dirt or oil.

Habb chabab ho natija leltgerat al-harmoniye fe al-jassam alti tespeb metabat al-hamra walbida sghira thadth ola allouge. Yamken asada an yakon sebeb habb chabab minn qabel al-raya ger al-salima lojhai methel turakem al-awsakh oa naft.

The most common form of acne is called pimples. They are small bumps that appear on the surface of your skin on the face with a white top. Touching it will only cause it to be inflamed, so refrain from touching your face.

Wisme shakl al-akthar shioa minn habb chabab al-bathour. Wahi ntoat saghira tadhar ola sith bochortak ola allouge baali abid. Lemse lin yode ella elly an yakon milthabah ledlec lamtna an lems weghk

Washing your face with soap is an effective way to reduce acne. With soap, gently lather your face before rinsing it off with water daily. If you do not have soap, rinsing your face with only water will do.

Gessel weghk balsabon ho wassila faala lalhad minn habb chabab. Ma sabune raghwa baltof weghk qabel al-rayneh qabala balma yomia. Idha lem yakan ledic sabune wallerins weghk balma fakt sov tafal.
