Girl's Puberty

Al-Bloug Lovteat

Puberty occurs for girls between the ages of 8 and 13.
This is when your body is transitioning into one of a woman. Here are the different ways your body is changing:

Wihas al-bloug lovteat al-lati tatraweh amarhan pen 8 senouat wa 13 sanna.
Hudha ho andama jasmak ho alantqal elly vahida minn amra. Fima lelle al-turq al-mukhtalafa alti yetger baha jasmak:

  • Periods: please refer to the Adults Health: Women section.

    Al-Fatrat: Yergi al-rajou elly saha al-baleghin: Qassem al-nasaa.

  • Growth Spurts: all of a sudden, the boys you were usually taller than or catching up to you! Not to worry, boys are generally taller than girls. You may notice that you are growing, and you may notice that you are not. Your height is entirely dependent on those of your parents, so however tall they are, chances are you will be, too.

    • Tafarat Al-Namu: Fajaas waloulad kent adeh attol minn oa al-lahaq beck! La daei laglaqalqa al-awlad hem amoma attol minn fittiat. Qud tlahez ank tanmou waqder tlahez ank list keddelk. Artfaa al-khas beck yatamad clea ola telk minn waldeke ledlec mhama kant towela vahnak ahtamalat sov tecone asada.

  • Body Chances: at this stage, you will begin to develop breasts. Do not be alarmed, this is completely natural. You may notice how you are gaining a little bit more curves along your hips and chest, and this is also normal. Your hips may or may not be growing wider - this is dependent on your genetics.

    • Faras Al-Jassam: Fe huthe marhala sov tabadaa fe tetuir al-thadien. La tanzage ohada amar tabiai tamama. Qud tlahez keef yemkenk al-hassol ola menhaniat akther kalila ola tol ourkin walsadreh ohada amar tabiai asada. Qud yenmo ourkin oa la yenmoan ola nataq osa - ohada yatamad ola alam al-waratha al-khas beck.

  • Body Hair: you will see how more hair is growing in your armpits and down there. Do not panic! This is a natural process.

    • Shar Al-Jassam: Setre keef yenmo al-mazid minn al-shaar fe al-ibtin wasfel hanak. La daei ladaar! huthe amlia tabiaia.
