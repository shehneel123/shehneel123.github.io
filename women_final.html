Pregnancy

Al-Hamal

Pregnancy is a nine month period of the mother carrying a developing baby inside of her womb. It is important to eat plenty of nutrition, such as vegetables, fruits, and drink water to keep hydrated.

  • Sometimes, while a baby is developing, it may run into challenges that can result in an untimely death. When a baby dies inside of the womb, it is called a miscarriage. During this, the mother begins to heavily bleed, experience severe cramping, have back pain, and possibly develop a fever. If this occurs, do not panic. Let it pass, and bear through the pain. It hurts, but you will heal.

  • Sometimes, a baby is born early. This is called a premature birth. Babies that are premature are often small and tend to be weaker than fully developed babies. They are easily prone to infections, so keeping a healthy surrounding is important.

Menstruation

Al-Hayed

Menstruation, or more commonly known as periods, is the flow of blood from the uterus that occurs for several days if the female is not pregnant. Periods vary from girl to girl; for some, the flow of the blood is heavy and for others, it is light. For some girls, the time of the period and the number of days it last are the same, but for other girls, they can go months without having their period. This is called irregularity.

Acne

Habb Chabab

Acne is the result of hormonal changes in the body that causes small red and white bumps to occur on the face. Acne can also be caused by improper care of the face, such as a buildup of dirt or oil.

Habb chabab ho natija leltgerat al-harmoniye fe al-jassam alti tespeb metabat al-hamra walbida sghira thadth ola allouge. Yamken asada an yakon sebeb habb chabab minn qabel al-raya ger al-salima lojhai methel turakem al-awsakh oa naft.

The most common form of acne is called pimples. They are small bumps that appear on the surface of your skin on the face with a white top. Touching it will only cause it to be inflamed, so refrain from touching your face.

Washing your face with soap is an effective way to reduce acne. With soap, gently lather your face before rinsing it off with water daily. If you do not have soap, rinsing your face with only water will do.
