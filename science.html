Science

Al-Aloum

The world around us is composed of science, which is the study of the structure and behaviour of the physical and natural world through observation and experiment. Around you, there is always science occuring. Here are some examples:

Yatkon alalem minn holna minn al-alame al-dhay ho durasa benia waslock alalem al-madi waltbiei minn khallal al-moraqba waltgerba. Minn hulki hanak daema alam yahdeth. oufima lelle baad al-amthla:

  • Seasons: there are four seasons that happen. Summer, which is a hot climate. Fall, which is a cool climate, and this is when the leaves begin to fall from trees and are preparing for winter. Winter, which is the coldest part of the year, and it snows during this time. Spring, which is the warm part of the year, and everything is reviving and transitioning to summer.

    • Al-Fassawal: hanak arbaa mouassem alti thadth. As-saifue waho manach har. Al-kharife waho manach bardes ohada ho andama tabadaa al-oraq fe ankhfadh minn al-ashjar wetsted lafsal shatta. El-shatta al-dhay ho abard jaza minn al-sanas wanh al-thalough khallal hudha al-waqqat. Al-rabie al-dhay ho al-jaza al-dafi minn al-sanas wakl shaya ho ihia wallantqal elly al-saif.

  • Plants: the way plants grow, with soil, sun, and water, is science. Trees, grass, and all flowers are considered plants.

    • Nabatatat: altrega alti tenmo baha al-nabatati ma al-turba walshams walma ho alam. Whitber al-ashjar walashp wajmia al-zahour nabatatat.

  • Animals: there are so many animals around the world: in trees, forests, oceans, jungles, and other places.

    • Al-Haywanat: hanak al-kathir minn al-haywanat fe doumai anhaa alalem: fe al-ashjar walgabat al-mahitat waladgalle wamaken akhra. (beldan carman al-muhait al-hadi)
